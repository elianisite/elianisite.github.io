About Us

Eliani Studios is a regenerative creative agency and production house redefining how the world creates. We connect global brands with photographers, directors, and production teams rooted in their communities.

Our mission is to redistribute creative opportunity worldwide. By reimagining how productions are built, we move creativity beyond traditional hubs and into the hands of artists everywhere. Accessibility isn’t an afterthought—it’s our foundation, giving creators global reach and brands the ability to produce flexible, ethical, and sustainable campaigns anywhere.

With a history marked by systemic imbalance and limited access, the creative industry has long been concentrated in major cities. Eliani Studios is redefining its future, one that evolves through our living, breathing example.

Non-Negotiables

Contractual Integrity and Creative Rights

All contracts must comply with labor laws and industry regulations, ensuring fair, transparent terms and the elimination of exploitative clauses. Artists retain full ownership of their intellectual property. No work may be used, altered, or distributed without explicit consent and fair compensation.

Fair Pay and Equal Representation

Working with local artists or artists from different regions must never justify underpayment or exploitation. All fees must meet industry standards, ensuring equal pay for equal work. Representation must remain free from discrimination based on race, gender, age, nationality, or any other protected characteristic.

Respectful Working Conditions

All artists must be treated with dignity and professionalism at every stage of a project. Working environments must be safe, inclusive, and free from harassment, coercion, or abuse. Clear communication, mutual respect, and accountability are required from all collaborators.

OUR THREE PILLARS

Earth, Heart and Art

Earth

The Earth is a relative, not a resource.

Because we work across the globe, we remain accountable to the land we create on.

We care for the world, so we care for the Earth.

Heart

Empathy is the thread that draws us closer to the artists and clients we are grateful to encounter, it turns collaboration into evolution.

We move with a genuine love for the creative industry, not only for what it creates, but for the people and stories that live within it.

It is through this love that we seek to shape and evolve it, guiding it toward something more human, more inclusive, and more reflective of the world we believe is possible.

Art

As you may have noticed, the language of our three pillars would not exist without art... quite literally, it lives within them (heART, eARTh) an undeniable truth that creation sits at the core of everything.

Art is manifestation. It is the act of bringing the unseen into form, of shaping feeling, vision, and truth into something that can be shared, felt, and remembered. It is a kind of magic, a world-shaping craft that holds the power to shift perspective and reimagine what is possible.

When you work with Eliani, you are not just creating art. You are actively shaping the future: manifesting a world that is more just, more conscious, and undeniably more beautiful.
